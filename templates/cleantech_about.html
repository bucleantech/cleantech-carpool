About RhettRides

Where sustainability meets student needs, Rhett Rides was born.

Commuting home has never been a unique experience to members of the Boston University community. Traveling by car is one of the most convenient modes of travel for students making their way to and from campus. It is also, however, one of the most environmentally unfriendly.

To reduce our carbon footprint by mitigating the amount of unnecessary vehicular travel, Rhett Rides provides BU students a platform for finding peers that are driving to the same cities and towns as them so that they can carpool, sharing the environmental expense of the ride.
